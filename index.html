About Me

I am a dedicated Geomatics Engineer currently pursuing my Master’s degree at the University of Calgary. My expertise and interests lie in GNSS, GIS, photogrammetry, and Python programming. Passionate about spatial data analysis and geospatial technologies, I am always eager to explore innovative solutions in mapping, positioning, and remote sensing. With a strong technical background and problem-solving skills, I aim to contribute to advancements in geospatial science and technology.

Interest

Satellite Positioning

My love for GNSS and satellite positioning stems from a deep fascination with how invisible signals from space can precisely determine locations on Earth. This technology has transformed the field of Geomatics Engineering, offering powerful tools that bring accuracy, efficiency, and reliability to spatial data collection.

Remote Sensing and GIS

My passion for GIS (Geographic Information Systems) and Remote Sensing comes from their power to transform complex spatial data into meaningful insights. These technologies allow us to visualize, analyze, and understand the Earth in ways that were once unimaginable — all from a digital workspace or a satellite’s perspective.

Programming

My journey into programming began with curiosity — but it quickly evolved into a powerful toolset that complements my passion for geomatics. Among all languages, Python stands out for its simplicity, versatility, and robust ecosystem, making it a go-to choice for spatial analysis and GNSS data processing. Python bridges the gap between raw geospatial data and meaningful insights. From building custom map applications to automating GNSS workflows, Python empowers geomatics engineers to push the limits of spatial intelligence.

Entrepreneurship

I learned the spirit of entrepreneurship from my mum — a resilient, hardworking woman who taught me the value of perseverance, community, and turning challenges into opportunities. With her guidance and support, I started my very first venture: selling palm oil. That experience gave me hands-on insight into the basics of business — managing supply, building trust with customers, and learning how to adapt and grow.
